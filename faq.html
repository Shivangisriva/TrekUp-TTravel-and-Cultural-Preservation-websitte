  • Some of the top things to do in India include visiting the Taj Mahal in Agra, trekking in the Himalayas, experiencing the backwaters of Kerala, attending the Pushkar Camel Fair, and exploring the cultural diversity of cities like Jaipur and Varanasi.
    Adventure lovers can enjoy trekking in Himachal Pradesh, white-water rafting in Rishikesh, paragliding in Bir Billing, scuba diving in the Andaman Islands, or camel safari in the Thar Desert.
    India's cultural experiences include attending the Holi and Diwali festivals, exploring ancient temples like the temples of Khajuraho, visiting the vibrant markets of Rajasthan, and watching classical dance performances like Kathakali and Bharatanatyam.
    India is home to some of the world's most famous wildlife sanctuaries, including Jim Corbett National Park, Ranthambore National Park, Kaziranga National Park, and Sundarbans for tiger safaris, elephant sightings, and bird watching.
    Luxury experiences in India include staying in heritage palaces in Rajasthan, exploring the backwaters of Kerala on a private houseboat, enjoying a royal experience at the Oberoi hotels, and embarking on the Palace on Wheels train journey.
    Popular beach destinations include Goa, Kerala’s Varkala and Kovalam beaches, the Andaman Islands, Rishikesh, and the lesser-known beaches of Gokarna in Karnataka.
    Off-the-beaten-path destinations include Spiti Valley in Himachal Pradesh, Ziro Valley in Arunachal Pradesh, Tawang in Arunachal Pradesh, and the Rann of Kutch in Gujarat.
    Indian cuisine is diverse and flavorful. Some must-try dishes include butter chicken, biryani, dosa, samosas, pav bhaji, and sweets like gulab jamun and jalebi.
    India is generally safe for tourists, but like in any other country, it's important to take precautions. Stay in well-known areas, avoid isolated places at night, and be cautious with your belongings.
